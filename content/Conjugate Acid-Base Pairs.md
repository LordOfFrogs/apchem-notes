2 species related to each other by 1 $\text{H}^+$ 
One species will be [[Acid]], and one [[Base]]
[[Acid]] formed from [[Base]] gaining proton
[[Base]] formed from [[Acid]] losing proton
# Examples
| [[Acid]]               | [[Base]]             |
| ---------------------- | -------------------- |
| $\text{HA}$            | $\text{A}^-$         |
| $\text{H}_3\text{O}^+$ | $\text{H}_2\text{O}$ |
| $\text{HCl}$           | $\text{Cl}^-$        |
